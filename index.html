I then challenged myself by taking part in a startup property management company, where I moved to Colorado to work for Avanti Residential. My career path has been diverse starting from dairy, to food production to property management. Through these different fields I have always kept learning and excelling. I have loved my past fields, as they have led me to my next opportunity, but I cannot wait to start a new chapter within the tech industry.
